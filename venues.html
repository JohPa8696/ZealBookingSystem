Gallery Space:
We have an awesome gallery space by the entrance of the venue where you can exhibit your work. The exhibition can be seen from outside the building looking through the glass wall.
You can use our cafe space or the main auditorium to host exhibition openings and artist talks.

Meeting Room:
We have a dedicated meeting room that can be accessed from a separate entrance. It can be used as boardroom style or theatre style - it is very user friendly and will meet your various hui requirements. It has tables, chairs and a whiteboard and Tv screen can be used if requested. It has a kitchen facility within the room.

We have an onsite cafe operated by our young people who are learning barista skills where you can purchase hot food and drinks . Let us know if you would like us to keep the cafe open during your event.
(All of the profits from the cafe will go to support our youth programmes)
