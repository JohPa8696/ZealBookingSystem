Looking for a place to rehearse or jam?

Our rehearsal rooms in West Auckland are purpose-built and cheap to hire! All rooms are on the ground floor with easy access for loading gear in through our side entrance from the carpark. (No stairs!)

The Zeal studios are open for Rehearsal bookings Monday to Friday evenings 6-10pm.
If you would like to book rehearsal rooms outside our open hours or recording sessions the times are available upon request.

We have 3 fully sound proofed rehearsal rooms all named after Kiwi bands or Artists.
“Six60” room! our largest room that fits up to 10 people.
“Crowded house” room! Great for a 4-5 piece bands.
“Lorde” room! Great for a 4-5 piece bands.

All rehearsal rooms have…
2 x Electro-Voice ZLX-15” 2-way powered Loudspeakers
Behringer 24 bit Multi FX Processor- 12 channel audio mixing Desk
1x Full Drum Kit “Mapex brand”- BYO Cymbals/Hi Hats
1 x SM58 Microphone
1 x Bass Amp
1 x Guitar Amp

Plus all necessary cables included in the room.
Additional Microphones, Cymbals/hi hat sets, drum sticks and Amps are available to hire.

Looking to record your band's EP or your demo tracks?

We have a small team of qualified Audio Engineers who are starting out in the industry and keen as to record your Music and help you share it with the world! We have a purpose built studio control room with vocal booth. The control room is connected to the rehearsal rooms so you can record utilising multiple live rooms at the same time.
